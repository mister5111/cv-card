My Story

I started my journey in IT in 2013. I started as a young IT engineer and gained experience from my senior colleagues.

Having gained some experience, I got bored in the company, I realized that I was "standing still" and there was no development. Having changed the company, I got a chance to rely only on my experience and knowledge. I ended up in a company where there was no IT infrastructure at all, one PC in the closet was all that was there... a router as an Internet gateway... I won't even mention security...

A server was rented from the Hetzner hosting company. A vmware esxi server was installed, a domain controller, a terminal server were raised, PfSense + Snort were used as an Internet gateway, the Internet gateway equipment was replaced with mikrotik at the branches, VPN tunnels were built, and a Zabbix server monitoring was added, and veeam backup was configured on Hetzner storage. This is how I consolidated the experience I gained in my previous company.

The next stage in my career I was involved in the telephone company and mikrotik a lot of mikrotik... The company went bankrupt... Without sitting still, I started developing myself... With my new knowledge, I was invited to the position of DevOps.

I am always interested in new projects and ideas, so if your company has an open position for DevOps, I would be happy to discuss contact me.
